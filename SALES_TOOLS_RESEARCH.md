# CarpetCRM Sales Tools Research
## For John's Flooring Shop — June 2026

---

## 1. Room Visualisation (The Closer)

**What it does:** Customer uploads a photo of their room, overlays carpet/LVT on the floor in real-time.

| Tool | Price | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CarpetCRM Built-in** | Free | Basic overlay | Already in your CRM — upload photo, select product, canvas render |
| **Roomvo** | £200-500/mo | High-end shops | Industry leader — photorealistic, huge product library |
| **Floorplanner** | Free-£50/mo | 2D/3D room plans | Customer draws room, fits carpet, sees 3D walkthrough |
| **MeasureSquare** | £100-300/mo | Commercial/retail | Mobile measure + visualise + quote in one |
| **Quick-Step Visualizer** | Free (brand locked) | Specific brands | Only works with their products |

**Verdict for John:** CarpetCRM's built-in visualiser is fine for now. If he wants photorealistic, **Roomvo** is the gold standard but expensive. **Floorplanner** is the best free alternative for 3D room planning.

## 3. Customer Financing (The Deal Closer)

**The single biggest sales tool:** Let customers pay monthly.

| Provider | Setup Cost | Customer Rate | John's Fee | Min Order |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Klarna** | Free | 0% (3 payments) | 2.9% + 20p | £35 |
| **DivideBuy** | Free | 0% (up to 12mo) | 3-5% | £200 |
| **PayPal Credit** | Free | 0% (4 months) | ~3% | £99 |
| **Snap Finance** | Free | High (bad credit OK) | 5-8% | £250 |
| **NewDay (Breathe)** | Free | 0% (up to 24mo) | 4-6% | £500 |

**Verdict:** **Klarna** for small orders, **DivideBuy** for larger installations. Snap Finance for customers with bad credit (huge market in flooring). John needs at least one of these on his quotes.

## 4. Review Generation (Social Proof Machine)

| Tool | Price | How It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Business** | Free | Ask happy customers to leave Google review |
| **BirdEye** | £200/mo | Auto SMS after install → review request → monitor all platforms |
| **Trustpilot** | £150/mo | Public review page, badges for website |
| **Review.io** | £50/mo | Cheaper BirdEye alternative |
| **CarpetCRM Built-in** | Free | Manual "Request Review" button, tracks pending |

**Verdict:** Start with **Google Business** (free, local SEO gold). Upgrade to **Review.io** or **BirdEye** when he's doing 10+ jobs/week.

## 5. WhatsApp Business API

**What John's missing:** Automated customer comms.

| Feature | Free WhatsApp | WhatsApp Business API |
|---|---|---|
| Broadcast messages | 256 contacts | Unlimited |
| Auto-replies | Basic | Advanced workflows |
| Quote links | Manual paste | Auto-generate + track clicks |
| Templates | None | Pre-approved message templates |
| Cost | Free | ~£0.03/message |

**Use cases for John:**
- "Hi Sarah, your measure is booked for Thursday 10am with Steve"
- "Your quote is ready: [link]. Tap to accept or call us."
- "Follow-up: How are you finding the samples?"
- "2 weeks post-install: Leave us a review? [Google link]"

**Verdict:** CarpetCRM already generates quote links. John needs **WhatsApp Business API** to send them at scale. Providers: **Twilio** (£), **MessageBird** (££), **360dialog** (cheapest).

## 6. Lead Generation

| Channel | Cost | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Local Services Ads** | £15-30/lead | "Carpet fitter near me" — pay per lead, not click |
| **Facebook/Instagram Ads** | £5-10/day | Visual ads — before/after room shots |
| **Checkatrade/MyBuilder** | £50-100/mo + commission | Lead marketplace, builds trust |
| **Nextdoor** | £20/mo | Local community, high trust |
| **Google Business Profile** | Free | Local pack ranking, photos, reviews |

**Verdict:** **Google Local Services** is the highest intent (people actively searching). **Facebook/Instagram** for visual inspiration. **Checkatrade** for credibility. Start with Google Business (free) + Local Services (£).
